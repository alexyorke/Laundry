Laundry Visualization

Vertical axis = % capacity in use

Binghamton's laundry machines are hooked up to eSuds to check availability remotely. However, there was no way to view historical data or decide when would be best to do your laundry overall. I wrote a simple scraper to log laundry capacity in Newing. This collected over 60,000 data points over several months.
